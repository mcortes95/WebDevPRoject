Description

We are a group of students who are passionate about video games and programming and who want to add our own fresh new ideas into the world.

Meetings

We meet once a week, 4:00pm on Wednesdays in room 107 of the Fine Arts Building. We usually discuss and plan any ongoing projects. This includes assigning current members specific tasks for the project.

Be sure to be included in the email newsletter to stay up to date on upcoming events or incase a meeting is cancelled or altered

If for whatever reason you aren't receiving our emails or just wish to not sign up, all current information will be included in the News page

Signing up

If you're interested in signing up, you can either click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation bar and complete the form, or you can visit our club during one of our scheduled meeting times.
