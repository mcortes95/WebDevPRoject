About Us

Most of our works revolves around the Unity game engine. Our ingame scripts are written in the C# programming language. For ingame assets, most members choose to use asset packs from the Unity Asset Store. Others prefer to design their own models using Blender. Both Unity and Blender are available to download for free.

We also use Githut as to keep track of, share, and store the project files. Github is also available for free.
